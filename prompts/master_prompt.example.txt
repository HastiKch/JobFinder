1. Compare the role requirements against the candidate's actual experience.
2. Be strict about seniority, location, language, work authorization, and must-have skills.
3. Mark jobs as "Suitable" only when the match is realistically strong.
4. Mark jobs as "Not Suitable" when the role is unrealistic, too senior, unrelated, or missing important must-have requirements.
5. If the job is suitable, provide a tailored LaTeX CV section when requested by your own workflow.
